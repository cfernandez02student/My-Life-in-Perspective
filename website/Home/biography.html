Cristina is a Research Intern at Saturday Night Live at NBC Universal assisting writers and cast members. As brand ambassador at Red Bull North America, she interacts with the public, informing them about the Red Bull brand.  

Cristina holds a Bachelor of Arts in Broadcasting Communications from Manhattan College in New York City.  She also has a minor in Digital Media Arts focusing on graphic design, digital drawing, digital photography and photoshop.  

In addition to her experience in marketing and entertainment broadcasting, Cristina is a video editor using Avid Media Composer, Final Cut Pro, and Social Media.

She enjoys using her public speaking and leadership skills as a dance instructor for Universal Dance Association Summer Camps for high school students.
